Durable Furniture with Aluminum Profiles

Aluminum Profiles July 18, 2023
Read Article

Aluminum profiles have revolutionized modern furniture design, offering unparalleled strength-to-weight ratios, sleek aesthetics, and exceptional durability. Mexis aluminum framing systems provide architects and furniture makers with versatile solutions for creating structures that stand the test of time.

Advantages of Aluminum Profile Furniture

Aluminum extrusion profiles offer numerous benefits over traditional wood or steel furniture frames:

  • Exceptional durability - Resistant to corrosion, rust, and warping
  • Lightweight strength - High strength-to-weight ratio for easy handling
  • Design flexibility - Extruded into nearly any shape for custom applications
  • Low maintenance - Doesn't require painting or special treatments
  • Eco-friendly - 100% recyclable with minimal environmental impact
  • Modern aesthetics - Clean lines and contemporary appearance

Design Insight

Combine aluminum profiles with glass, wood, or stone surfaces to create stunning visual contrasts. The metallic finish of aluminum pairs beautifully with both warm natural materials and cool industrial surfaces.

Surface Finish Options

Our aluminum profiles come in various finishes to match your design requirements:

  1. Mill Finish - Natural aluminum appearance with slight texture
  2. Anodized - Electrochemical process creating durable colored surfaces
  3. Powder Coated - Wide color selection with excellent scratch resistance
  4. Brushed - Linear grain pattern for sophisticated look
  5. Polished - Mirror-like reflective surface

Installation Techniques

Proper assembly ensures structural integrity and longevity of aluminum profile furniture:

  • Corner Brackets - For 90° joints with hidden fasteners
  • T-Slot Nuts - Allow adjustable connections along profile length
  • End Caps - Provide finished appearance and safety
  • Gusset Plates - Reinforce high-stress joints
  • Threaded Inserts - Create durable threaded points in profiles

Technical Note

When designing with aluminum profiles, account for thermal expansion (23.1 μm/m·°C). For outdoor applications in variable climates, leave 1-2mm expansion gaps per meter length at joints.

Maintenance Guidelines

While aluminum requires minimal maintenance, proper care extends its lifespan:

  • Cleaning - Use mild soap solution and soft cloth; avoid abrasive cleaners
  • Scratch Prevention - Use felt pads under objects on aluminum surfaces
  • Anodized Care - Periodic waxing maintains protective oxide layer
  • Powder Coat Repair - Touch-up pens available for minor damage
  • Hardware Check - Tighten connections annually in high-use furniture
