Our Growth Journey

From a Surat startup to a national presence, our story is one of innovation and dedication to "Maximum Ease"

2021

The Beginning

Surat, Gujarat

Mexis was founded with a clear vision: to create hardware solutions that bring "Maximum Ease" to furniture functionality. Starting from a small workshop in Surat, we began crafting premium Indian-made hardware.

2022

Gujarat Expansion

Across Gujarat

Our commitment to quality and innovation quickly gained recognition. Within a year, we expanded across Gujarat, establishing partnerships with furniture manufacturers throughout the state.

Key Achievements:
  • Launched first product catalog
  • Established 5-city distribution
  • Team grew to 15 members
2022

National Recognition

Western & Northern India

Demand for our "Maximum Ease" solutions grew beyond Gujarat. We expanded to Maharashtra, Rajasthan, and Delhi NCR, becoming a nationally recognized hardware brand.

New Locations:
Mumbai Pune Jaipur Delhi Ahmedabad
2023

Nationwide Presence

25+ Cities Across India

Today, Mexis serves clients in over 25 cities nationwide, staying true to our "Maximum Ease" philosophy while continuously innovating our product line.
