Smart Kitchen Storage with Tandem Systems

Modern kitchens demand storage solutions that combine functionality with elegance. Mexis tandem drawer systems provide the perfect balance of silent operation, smooth movement, and space optimization to transform your kitchen experience.

Why Choose Tandem Systems for Your Kitchen?

Tandem drawer systems have become the gold standard for premium kitchen cabinetry. Unlike traditional drawer slides, our tandem systems offer:

  • 100% extension for complete access to drawer contents
  • Silent soft-close mechanism that prevents slamming
  • Heavy-duty capacity (up to 50kg per drawer)
  • Smooth ball-bearing operation for effortless movement
  • Concealed design that enhances cabinet aesthetics

Pro Tip

For optimal performance, pair our tandem systems with high-quality drawer boxes made from 18mm thick plywood or MDF. This ensures perfect alignment and maximizes the system's weight capacity.

Installation Best Practices

Proper installation ensures optimal performance and longevity of your tandem drawer systems:

  1. Measure accurately - Ensure cabinet interior dimensions match the drawer system length
  2. Level the cabinet - Use shims if necessary to ensure perfect alignment
  3. Mark positions carefully - Follow our template for precise runner placement
  4. Secure firmly - Use appropriate screws (included) for solid mounting
  5. Test movement - Check for smooth operation before finalizing installation

Maintenance and Care

To keep your tandem drawer systems performing like new:

  • Clean regularly - Wipe runners with a dry cloth to remove dust and debris
  • Lubricate annually - Apply silicone-based lubricant to moving parts
  • Check alignment - Tighten any loose screws and adjust as needed
  • Avoid overloading - Stay within the specified weight limits
