Welcome to Mexis

Innovative Solutions for Modern Living

Mexis is a leading manufacturer and supplier of innovative hardware solutions, specializing in premium products such as soft close channels, aluminum profiles, tandem systems, and advanced lock systems. With a strong focus on quality, durability, and cutting-edge functionality, we are committed to delivering the best in home furniture design, wardrobe solutions, and modern kitchen hardware.

At Mexis, we understand the importance of modern interior design and space optimization. Our products are meticulously designed to enhance the functionality and aesthetics of your living spaces, whether it's your kitchen, wardrobe, or office. From soft-close drawer systems to aluminum profiles for furniture, we offer a wide range of solutions that cater to both residential and commercial needs.

We take pride in our extensive network of over 30 companies across India, where we provide private label manufacturing services. Our ability to deliver custom hardware solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients has made us a trusted partner in the hardware manufacturing industry. Whether you're looking for kitchen tandem boxes, sliding fittings, or mortise locks, Mexis ensures the highest standards of design and engineering in every product.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Surat, Gujarat, is equipped with advanced technology and a skilled workforce, enabling us to maintain consistent quality and meet the growing demands of our clients. We are dedicated to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, making us a preferred choice for home furniture hardware and interior design solutions across India.
